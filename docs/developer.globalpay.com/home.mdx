>
    Use our API or an SDK to embed card-present payments into your POS device. Or, use our cloud service to meet all your business needs.
    * [**Terminals:**](/docs/payments/in-store/standalone) Available "out of the box" or integrated to your existing POS system, with do-business-anywhere connectivity.
    * [**Semi-Integration:**](/docs/payments/in-store/semi-integration) Pair your ePOS with an Android or Ingenico terminal, or find one from one of our integration partners. No <Tooltip content="Mandate required by Europay, MasterCard, and Visa (EMV) for payment acceptance systems.">EMV</Tooltip> certification needed.
    * [**Full Integration:**](/docs/payments/in-store/full-integration) Available "out of the box" or integrated to your existing POS system, with do-business-anywhere connectivity.
    * [**Cloud Integration:**](/docs/payments/in-store/cloud ) Handle your whole business, not just payments. Requires less code and certification than a traditional semi-integration.
</InStorePaymentsSection>
