<ReleaseNoteSection title="List Transactions">
    <ReleaseNoteDetail type="added">
        * New "platforms" object returned in the response List Transactions:
            * platforms.type
            * platforms.transaction_id
            * platforms.order_id
            * platforms.authcode
            * Platforms.pasref
    </ReleaseNoteDetail>
</ReleaseNoteSection>

<ReleaseNoteSection title="Funding Report">
    <ReleaseNoteDetail type="added">
        * New settlement funding endpoint
            * Funding reports include a summary of all deposits and withdrawals processed for the past day and month
    </ReleaseNoteDetail>
</ReleaseNoteSection>

<ReleaseNoteSection title="Create a Sale or Refund">
    <ReleaseNoteDetail type="added">
        * Card Present (Canada) now supports Pre-Authorization and Pre-Authorization Completion for Canada Interac cards
        * New validation for "encryption.method" field in the Reverse a Sale or Refund method
        * Click To Pay support
            * Click To Pay transactions are now supported:
                * New enum "CLICK_TO_PAY" added for digital_wallet.provider
                * New field "data" added in "digital_wallet.payment_token object"
        * Support for individual "charge_items" amount for Card Present (Asia Pacific)
            * Amounts can be sent for the below "charge_items" type:
                * RESTAURANT
                * GIFT_SHOP
                * MINI_BAR
                * PHONE
                * LAUNDRY
                * OTHER
                * NO_SHOW
    </ReleaseNoteDetail>
</ReleaseNoteSection>
